Iron Man

Iron Man is one of the most iconic superheroes from the Marvel Universe, created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 (1963).

Publication history

Premiere

Tony Stark is a billionaire engineer and the owner of Stark Industries. While demonstrating weapons overseas, he’s captured and severely injured. To survive, he builds a miniature arc reactor to power his heart and designs a metal suit to escape captivity. This becomes the first Iron Man armor.

Solo Series

The premiere received overwhelming praise from both critics and fans. The movie’s mix of action, humor, and character depth changed superhero cinema forever and launched one of the most successful film franchises in history.

Fictional character biography

Tony Stark is witty, confident, and sometimes arrogant, but he’s also deeply human — struggling with guilt, responsibility, and the desire to protect the world from his own creations.
